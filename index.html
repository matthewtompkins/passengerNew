This is Passenger.

Passenger is a new kind of support system based in Detroit, MI. Our ambition is to provide relief from the daily anxieties that accompany traveling while in recovery. Whether it's transportation to a meeting, a safe space beyond the green room, or our Compass interactive meeting database, we want you to know you're not alone. Passenger can help.

Our Story

passenger can help

transportation

Passenger Recovery offers a free transportation service to get you to and from meetings during your visit.

clean green room

The Clean Green Room project provides a safe, sober environment for travelers in recovery.

meetings

Passenger Recover hosts weekly 12-step meetings in Hamtramck, MI.

community services

Guest speakers, special events, and support for local arts encompass our additional services to the community.
